Roadmap of Cyber Security

  • A career in cyber security can be both rewarding and in demand in various industries. Here are some common career opportunities in cyber security field:

    • Cyber Security Skills:

  • (1)To become a cybersecurity expert, you should understand firewalls, VPN, Web-proxy, and IDS/IPS. (2) You need to be proficient with coding languages such as Java, Python, HTML, and PHP. (3) You should have an excellent knowledge of networking, routing protocols, and encryption techniques. (4) A cybersecurity practitioner should know the best practices for Identity and Access Management (IAM). (5) Cybersecurity professionals need to know SQL and Database management systems and possess problem-solving skills.

    • 1.Network Engineer Job Role

  • Network engineers construct a company's computer networks. They install, configure, and support network equipment. They also configure firewalls, switches, and routers. This entry-level cyber security job can also help you start your journey to become an Ethical Hacker..

    • 2. Information Security Analyst Job Role:

  • They ensure compliance with policies. Depending on the cyber threat, they decide if it must be resolved or escalated further. According to Indeed, an information security analyst earns $89,140 annually in the US, and in India, they earn ₹6,42,000..

    • 3. Ethical Hacker Job Role

  • They test the network using various software and penetration tools. They also design new penetration tools and document the test results. According to Payscale, a certified ethical hacker earns around $93,000 on an annual average basis in the US, and in India, they make about ₹5,00,000 rupees.

    • 4. Security Architect Job Role

  • Security architects research and propose the security elements for their organizations. They design robust security structures that are capable of preventing malware attacks. A security architect approves the installation of routers, VPN, and firewalls. They formulate company procedures, guidelines, and user guides.

    • Skills needed:

  • Advanced statistical modeling, machine learning, programming languages (Python, R), big data technologies (Hadoop, Spark).
